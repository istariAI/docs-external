Each cookbook takes a concrete question — *"Which French hotels still use contact forms?"*, *"Who makes heat
pumps in Baden-Württemberg?"* — and walks through the exact searches, filters, and exports to answer it.
Follow along in the [GOI dashboard](/goi/goi_start_page), or adapt the same steps to the [API](/api/api_start_page).
