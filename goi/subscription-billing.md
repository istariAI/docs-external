# Subscription and Billing

The **Subscription and Billing** page is where you track your credits, review invoices, and look back over the API calls and on-demand actions that have consumed credits.

To open it, click your **profile avatar** in the bottom-left of the sidebar and choose **Subscription and Billing** from the dropdown. You can also click the **credits balance** number under your avatar — it links straight here.

<figure><img src="/images/goi_billing_overview.png" alt="Subscription and Billing page"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Total credits

At the top of the page, the **Total Credits** card shows your current balance in euros. Credits are used for on-demand features such as API requests.

To the right of the page header is an **API pricing** button. Click it for a quick reference of how credits are consumed (for example, **€0.10 per company** returned through the API).

## Invoices

The **Invoices** tab lists your most recent invoices. Each row shows the date, amount, and status of the invoice so you can keep track of what has been billed.

## Transactions

The **Transactions** tab shows a detailed log of credit movements — for example, credits added through a top-up, or credits consumed by API requests. Use this view to reconcile usage against your invoices.

The Subscription and Billing page is only visible to accounts that have credit-based features enabled. If your plan doesn't include them, the menu entry won't appear in your profile dropdown.
