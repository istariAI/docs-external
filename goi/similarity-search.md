# Similarity Search

Similarity Search is one of the [three ways to search](/goi/dashboard#three-ways-to-search) in GOI. Use this method when you already know a **reference company** and want to find similar ones.

## How it works

* Switch the search type to **Use a similar website** (in Advanced mode).
* Enter a company's website, e.g. `bosch.de` or `vibracoustic.com`.
* ISTARI automatically analyses the company's public digital footprint and generates a feature-rich company summary.
* That summary is then used to scan our master database and return similar organizations.

This method is ideal for **competitive benchmarking**, **supplier identification**, or **peer group analysis**.

## Refining a similarity search

Once you've added a reference domain, you can steer the result set with up to **three "prioritize"** and **three "avoid"** keywords:

* **Add keywords to prioritize** — favour companies whose profiles mention these terms. Useful when the reference company has multiple business lines and you care about only one of them.
* **Add keywords to avoid** — downweight companies that mention these terms. Useful to exclude adjacent industries or irrelevant segments.

Click the **+** icon next to the domain chip to open the keyword menu, type a keyword and press **Enter** to add it as a pill. Green pills prioritize; red pills avoid. Remove any pill with its **×** button.

You can combine similarity search with **Location Filters** and **Other Filters** (keywords, organization size, type, and NACE) for very targeted peer group discovery.
