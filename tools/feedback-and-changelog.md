# Feedback & changelog

If you have **ideas, feedback**, or want to **suggest changes** to the platform, you can now do that easily on our public feedback board:

👉 [Submit Feedback or Suggestions Here](https://istariaimarkets.featurebase.app/de)

On that page, you can:

* 💡 Propose new features
* 🐞 Report bugs
* 👍 Vote on suggestions from other users
* 📢 Follow our [**changelog** ](https://istariaimarkets.featurebase.app/de/changelog)to stay updated on new releases and improvements

You can access our feedback and suggestions portal also directly via ISTARI Markets. Just click on the settings symbol in the bottom left-hand corner:
