# 🔑 Platform login

You can access the webAI platform via the web address: [https://dashboard.istari.ai](https://dashboard.istari.ai)

Your login details were sent to the email address (which is also your `Username`) you provided when you subscribed. Use this login data for your first login.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/login.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Login screen.</p></figcaption></figure>

After your first login you will be asked to set a new password. The password must meet the following criteria:

* At least one capital letter
* At least one lower case letter
* At least one number
* At least one special character

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/02_first_login.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Reset Password screen after first login.</p></figcaption></figure>

If you have troubles logging in or you forgot your password, please contact our support via: [support@istari.ai](mailto:support@istari.ai)
