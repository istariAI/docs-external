# 🧩 Modules overview

The modules on the webAI platform offer specific functions that allow you to process the company lists you have created.

## Available Modules

You can access the available modules, via the `Modules` section in the side panel. Please note that not every module is available for all subscriptions. You can load the corresponding module by clicking `Load Module`.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/31_modules.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Module overview page.</p></figcaption></figure>

## Load company lists

When you load a module, you will see the `Load Company List` drop-down menu at the top. By default, the last search you carried out ("Last Search") is already loaded there. Select the list you want to work on from the drop-down menu and then click on `Load Company List` to load it.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/32_load_company_list.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Load company list drop-down menu.</p></figcaption></figure>

Below the menu you will see how many companies are in the currently loaded list.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/33_loaded_list.png" alt=""><figcaption><p>Loaded list with 55 company entries.</p></figcaption></figure>
